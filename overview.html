Wordcloud of nations per life expectancy

We can visualize the quantity of trees by species thanks to the barchart. Since there are 224 tree species, it is reasonable to view only the 15 most numerous species.
Moving the mouse over the single bars, we can visualize the exact count and the average canopy size of each species.

Histogram of top and worst states per life expectancy

Now, let's analyze the amount of trees, taking into consideration the neighborhood in which they are located. The trees that did not belong to the top 5 species were grouped into the Others category. It's interesting to note that in the most numerous neighborhood, the most numerous tree species is not Celtis australis, even if it is the most numerous species among all the neighborhoods. As we can expect from the nature of the Others class, it is the most numerous in each circoscrizione.
Moving the mouse over a specific portion of a bar, we can visualize the exact amount of trees for the selected species in a district.

Parallel coordinates chart of alcohol consumption, life expectancy and scholarity index

We always analyze the number of trees in the various neighborhoods. The visualization via the small multiple stacked barchart, as it separates the tree types, help us to compare the amounts of a specific tree species with respect to the different districts they belong to.
